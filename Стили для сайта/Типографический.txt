Redesign the site in a dark typographic / editorial style.
Background: deep black (#0c0c0c) or dark ink (#111318).
Color palette: white (#ffffff), light gray (#aaaaaa), and one strong accent (electric red #ff2d2d or bright yellow #ffe600).
Typography is the hero: use large, bold display fonts (clash display, Bebas Neue, or Syne for headings; size 80–120px on desktop).
Body text: clean readable serif or sans (IBM Plex Serif or Neue Haas Grotesk).
Layout: text-heavy, editorial grid — asymmetric columns, oversized pull quotes, large letter-spacing on section labels.
No decorative illustrations — all visual interest comes from type scale, weight contrast, and layout.
Minimal imagery; if images used, they should be dark, high-contrast or duotone.
Apply to all typography, layout grid, and component hierarchy.
